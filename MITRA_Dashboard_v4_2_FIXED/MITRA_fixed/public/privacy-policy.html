Important: This Privacy Policy is written in plain language as required by the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India). It is also available in Hindi and other regional languages on request.

1. Introduction

MITRA is a government-aligned educational AR platform deployed across Indian schools. We are deeply committed to protecting the personal data of students, teachers, and administrators. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights under Indian law.

2. Who This Policy Applies To

This policy applies to:

  • Students — who use the MITRA mobile application
  • Teachers & District Officers — who access the administrative dashboard
  • School Administrators — who manage accounts and content
  • Parents/Guardians — of minor students

4. Data We DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ Precise GPS location of students
  • ❌ Student photographs or biometric data
  • ❌ Financial information
  • ❌ Communications content (chat, messages)
  • ❌ Social media profiles
  • ❌ Data from users under 13 years of age without parental consent

5. Legal Basis for Processing (DPDP Act 2023)

We process personal data under the following lawful bases as defined in the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023:

  • Consent — obtained at first login via a bilingual consent flow (Section 6)
  • Legitimate Use — for the purpose of government educational services (Section 7)
  • Legal Obligation — for CERT-In security reporting requirements

6. Children's Data (Minors under 18)

MITRA serves students, many of whom are minors. We comply with:

  • Section 9 of the DPDP Act 2023 — Parental/guardian consent for minors
  • We do not process children's data for profiling or behavioural advertising
  • School principals act as authorised guardians for data purposes within educational institutions

7. Data Sharing

We share data ONLY with:

  • Government Authorities — State Education Departments (as data fiduciaries)
  • Technology Partners — AWS/Google Cloud for hosting (data processing agreements in place)
  • CERT-In — Mandatory security incident reporting within 6 hours

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with advertisers or third-party marketers.

8. Data Security Measures

  • AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit
  • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for all dashboard administrators
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) with least-privilege principles
  • 180-day audit log retention as required by CERT-In 2022 directions
  • Regular penetration testing (quarterly)
  • Incident Response Plan documented in INCIDENT_RESPONSE.md

10. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Inactive accounts: Auto-purged after 12 months of inactivity
  • Deleted accounts: All personal data anonymised within 30 days of deletion request
  • Audit logs: Retained for 180 days as mandated by CERT-In directions (2022)
  • Anonymised analytics: May be retained indefinitely for research purposes

11. Cookies & Tracking

The MITRA Dashboard uses session cookies for authentication only. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. The student mobile app uses only essential functionality analytics.

12. Cross-Border Data Transfer

All personal data of Indian citizens is stored on servers physically located within India (Mumbai/Hyderabad AWS regions) in compliance with Section 16 of the DPDP Act 2023. No personal data is transferred outside India without explicit consent and data transfer agreements.
